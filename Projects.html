Project Details

1. Performance analysis of multilevel inverter with reduced number of switches

  • Simulated 2-level, 5-level, 7-level, 9-level, 11-level, and 15-level inverters, using MATLAB software.
  • Analyzed and Compared the THD of each level.
  • Observed that percentage of THD decreases as the level of inverters increase.
  • Observed the fundamental voltage at each level.
